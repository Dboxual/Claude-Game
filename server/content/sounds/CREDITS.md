# Audio credits

Every sound the game ships is listed here with its source and license.
**Policy: any sound without a provably safe license (original work, CC0, or
public domain) gets rejected — it does not go in this folder.** No sounds
are taken from other games (no MW2, no Call of Duty, no Capcom, no rips of
any kind) or from unlicensed libraries.

All current sounds are ORIGINAL: synthesized from sine waves and
deterministic noise by [tools/generate_sounds.cpp](../../../tools/generate_sounds.cpp)
in this repository. No recordings and no third-party samples were used as
input, so there is nothing to attribute upstream. As original work created
for this project, they are dedicated to the public domain under
[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).
Re-running the tool reproduces these exact files.

## Adding sounds later

A new sound may only be added together with a row in this table. Acceptable
sources, in order of preference:

1. **Original** — synthesized or recorded by a contributor for this project
   (mark as `original (CC0)` like above).
2. **CC0 / public domain** — link the exact source page and archive the
   license text; verify the uploader actually holds the rights.

Anything else — CC-BY without attribution wired up, CC-BY-NC, "free for
personal use", unknown provenance, or content ripped from another game —
is rejected on sight.
